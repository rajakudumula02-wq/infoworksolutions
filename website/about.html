Building the Future of Payer Innovation

We help dental insurance companies meet federal mandates and drive member engagement through intelligent API infrastructure

Our Mission

Infowork Solutions exists to solve a critical problem: dental insurance payers face mounting pressure to comply with federal interoperability mandates while simultaneously improving member engagement and preventive care outcomes.

We provide three independent API platforms that address these challenges head-on: CMS-compliant FHIR infrastructure, AI-powered dental image analysis, and automated member campaign tools.

Why We Built This

The dental insurance industry operates on razor-thin margins. Every dollar spent on claims is a dollar that could have been saved through preventive care. Yet members consistently skip preventive dental visits until problems become painful and expensive.

At the same time, payers are required to implement complex FHIR APIs to meet CMS mandates—a massive technical lift that diverts resources from innovation. We saw an opportunity to provide both compliance infrastructure and engagement tools in a single platform.

Our Approach

Unlike consultancies that build custom solutions from scratch, we deliver production-ready API platforms that can be deployed in weeks. Our FHIR infrastructure is multi-tenant, standards-compliant, and built specifically for payer workflows. Our dental AI analyzes images in 3 seconds—fast enough for real-time member engagement.

We understand that dental payers need reliability, compliance, and measurable ROI. Every feature we build is designed to either reduce claims costs or meet regulatory requirements.
